About Us

Who We Are

The HHS Medical Club is a student-led organization for anyone curious about healthcare, biology, and the human body. We are a community of students who want to explore medical concepts, share learning resources, and discover what paths a medical career can offer—all in a relaxed, collaborative environment.

Our Current Focus

Exploring Medical Topics

Diving into interesting case studies, anatomical reviews, and recent discoveries about the human body (like our article series!).

Building a Community

Creating a space where students with similar interests can connect, share study tips for science classes, and learn together.

Curating Resources

Gathering open-access resources, volunteer guidance, and study materials to help members think about their futures in medicine.

HHS Medical Club

Cultivating curiosity, collaboration, and foundational knowledge in clinical science for the next generation of healthcare leaders.
