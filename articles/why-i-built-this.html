Why I Built This: Demystifying the Medical Path for Students

By Grishma Timsina

The Overwhelming Maze of Healthcare

When I first started considering medicine and healthcare, I realized I had absolutely no idea where to start. It was incredibly difficult just figuring out what opportunities and resources were out there, and following through with them was a completely different story.

From the outside, the medical field looks like an overwhelming maze. Finding clear information on how to get started, what different roles do daily, and where to take that very first step is surprisingly difficult for a student. Because I have no healthcare workers in my family and have little to no connections, the lack of guidance made me feel completely isolated. There were moments where I genuinely thought, “Maybe this path just isn’t for me.”

This is the exact barrier that this website is built to break down. I also want to mention that this platform isn’t just a directory, but an exploratory page to discover different paths and test if medicine is for you. No student should have to give up on their dreams just because they don’t have the connections to navigate the large barrier. I want our peers to feel supported, get practical advice, and see exactly what paths are open to them without the stress of guessing.

Our Core Pillars and Goals

Though this website is in very early stages, we have ambitious goals and hope to be a practical and useful resource for all students and club members. I want this club to be a place where people can explore medical topics they are curious about, foster a community, and be a compilation of resources.

If you are in the same position I was before, you are the very reason I decided to create this platform. I want you to know that you are supported, and that you belong, even if you don’t feel like it sometimes.

The most important qualities to have when pursuing medicine is genuine passion, empathy, and a desire to learn. No matter what your family situation is, what your background is, or how many connections you have, this platform aims to support you to achieve your goals.

I invite you to use this website as a stepping stone to reach your goals. Reach out to me, ask for advice, and give feedback on what you wish to be improved. Use this resource to its fullest potential!
